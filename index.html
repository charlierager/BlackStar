about us

Black Star Creative Media is a creative video production and copywriting company based in North Yorkshire. We have over 20 years of experience working in the retail and entertainment sectors. We understand what it takes to create videos that engage and motivate audiences. We produce creative, engaging and emotive videos for corporate and retail brands. As well as having everything we need in-house we also work with other directors, producers, and copywriters.

meet our leader

Robert Cathcart has over 20 years experience in corporate, retail, and entertainment sectors. As creative director, he worked with some of the UK's most prestigious brands. From athletes and chefs to retailers and celebrities... on one memorable occasion, interviewing Michael Parkinson! But it doesn't stop there - his diverse skill set includes making videos for retailers such as Marks & Spencer, Hugo Boss, Timberland, Costa Coffee, Boots and WH Smith, among many others. He's created pitch videos and documents used in corporate campaigns for small businesses, entrepreneurs and multinational property developers, including Stadium and British Land.
