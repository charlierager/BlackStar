copywriting

Your business is built on words - web pages, ads, DM, events, even customers talking to each other, it's all down to the right words. One slip, one wrong sentence, and it could take years to rebuild a reputation - just ask any politician that's been caught unawares by undercover journalists! I'm here to help you with those words. I've written professionally for nearly 10 years, with clients as diverse as Meadowhall Shopping Centre and the NHS, so I think I have a good feel for who wants to hear what, and from whom. I know what works, and what doesn't, whether I'm writing hyperbolic copy advertising the latest offer from a high street retailer or translating the words of a civil engineer into layman's terms for a corporate video. Tone, tempo and tag-line - I can help you to find the right way to communicate your message.

Web

Google is watching your site, always watching! Not only is Google watching but it's also analyzing, checking and ranking too, so the copy on your website needs to be original, dynamic and customer friendly. You need to speak to your audience in a way they relate to while also making sure the search engines like what you're saying and how you're saying it. Black Star Creative Media are here to do that hard work for you.





Script

Online video, corporate films, adverts or training scripts- these are all very important for getting your message across in the right way. With over 25 years of experience in Supermall TV, corporate B2B and training videos we know exactly what we are doing. We'll work with you to ensure the tone, the message and, most importantly, the words say exactly what you need them to.





Print

We all know how important good printed copy is, no one wants to go viral for all wrong reasons - an errant comma, an embarrassing typo, the wrong phrase in the wrong context. We can make sure all the printed media you supply is as dynamic and eye-catching as all of your online content.
