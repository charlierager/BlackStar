Premier Range is the largest supplier of glass splashbacks and matching accessories in the UK. Black Star Creative Media have worked with Premier Range for over 15 years, writing copy for their website, eBay stores and Amazon as well as press releases and personal correspondence. GasKing

GasKing was the brainchild of brothers Jamie and Adam, who saw an opportunity to improve the way gas central heating and plumbing services were provided. Jamie is a GasSafe engineer with 30 years of experience in the sector and, while working on new boiler installations and boiler repairs, he was sure he could offer a much better experience for customers and their families with a strong team behind him. This is where younger brother, Adam, came in. Adam is an entrepreneur with experience, and success, in everything from finance to football. We work very closely with Adam on SEO-optimised web copy, creating hundreds of pages to target local customers and provide a close bond between GasKing and their service users. We are also creating blogs and videos to further SEO optimise the site and ensure GasKing becomes the number one choice for plumbing and gas services in London and the surrounding area.
