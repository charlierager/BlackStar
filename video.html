production

Making videos, films or social media content isn't just a case of pointing a camera at something and pressing play. There are many elements that need coordinating and sorting out. These could include information, images, logos, venues, actors, presenters and any number of different variables. Well, here at Black Star Creative Media we will sort all that out for you. We'll book the talent, manage the budget, speak to the techs, liaise with you and make sure the whole process is as headache free as it can possible be.

script

Writing a script for a video or film is a different process to writing copy. We are more interested in directly engaging the viewer so we have to take into account how the information is presented and where the we take the viewer. Scripts will have camera angles, locations and notes for the director/presenter. We have been writing corporate, training and advertising scripts for over 25 years. We also have experience working with Clearcast, which is the independent body that must clear any adverts that will appear on television.

camera

As well as having in house camera operators to keep costs down we also have trusted freelancers on our books. We only use people we know and trust, people who have worked with us before and also freelance for the NHS, University and the BBC.

direct

We have your direction covered too! Directors are generally needed for bigger jobs that require actors, presenters or extensive filming. Testimonials, interviews, even factory walk throughs need someone to hold the whole show together and maintain a sense of how the end product will look. Our directors will be there for you no matter how big or small the job.
